About Me

The Artist Behind
The Models

Hey, I'm Mushy — a 3D artist who's been modeling since the pandemic hit. What started as a creative escape quickly became a full-blown passion. Over the years, I've honed my skills in 3D modeling, building, and rigging, delivering work that ranges from clean and simple to intricate and high-detail.

I specialize in creating weapons, props, characters, armor sets, and full environments with a focus on quality and detail. Whether you need a quick asset or a complex masterpiece, I bring the same level of dedication to every project.

FAQ

Rates &
Info

What are your rates?

Rates depend on the scope of the project. Bulk commissions get a lower price, while one-time commissions are average rate. High quality / complex work costs $100 – $150 USD (or 45k – 50k Robux).

How fast is your turnaround?

My fastest bulk commission was 50 weapons in 5 days. Realistically, turnaround is 1 – 3 days for bulk work. Complex or single commissions may take longer depending on detail.

How does payment work?

Usually 50% upfront / 50% upon completion. For customers who prefer it, I also accept full payment upon completion — trust goes both ways.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept PayPal, Wise, Gcash, and BDO. We can discuss which works best for you.
